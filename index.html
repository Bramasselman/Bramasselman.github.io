ROADEF 2009 Challenge

This website provides clarification and support for the final presentation of the course Airline Schedule Exercise. First of all a Logbook is included in which intermediate steps are provided, each of them containing a dataframe with relevant data. Next, the final schedules can be found in the tab Flight Schedule. Finally, the shortest path algorithm used for this assignment is described and visualised in Shortest Path.
